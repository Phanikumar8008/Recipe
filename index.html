Hyderabadi Veg Dum Biryani

Hyderabadi Dum Biryani is a popular spice and vegetables mixed favoured rice dish which is typically prepared by layering the biryani gravy and basmati rice in flat bottom vessel.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups water
  • 2 bay leaf / tej patta
  • 1 inch cinnamon stick
  • ½ tsp cloves
  • Chopped vegetables
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • 2 tsp oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1½ basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp oil

Why Biryani ?

Biryani is prepared from basmati rice mixed with several spices and cooked in a special way. Chicken Biryani, Mutton Biryani and Veg Biryani are some of the variants of this delicious dish. This dish is my favourite because it has a lip-smacking flavour, and I love to eat it at least once a week.
